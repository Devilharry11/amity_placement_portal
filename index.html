Placement Safety & Fraud Awareness

Identify Fraud Internships:
Verify official company website, avoid registration fees, and check email domain authenticity.
Verify Placement Calls:
Confirm job listing on official career page. Never share OTP or bank details.
Protect Your Privacy:
Do not share Aadhaar, PAN or sensitive documents in early hiring stages.
Report Suspicious Activity:
Inform placement cell and use official cyber crime reporting channels if required.
